Our Mission

Panafrica International has a focus on showcasing the strength and intelligence of different communities on the continent of Africa. We strive to bring business and public relations opportunities to the forefront of those countries. With your best interest in hand, Panafrica International has a focus on creating a better relationship and image of your country in the United States.

Our mission includes, but is not limited to, increasing foreign aid, bringing more U.S companies to African countries, promoting the beauty of your land with tourism and elevating the exposure of African companies.

Panafrica International is incorporated in the State of Ohio with connections throughout the United States of America and multiple African countries.
